# Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Current Date]

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to the St. Vincent School Foundation, Inc. (SVSFI) Online Enrollment System. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our online enrollment system.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us when using the enrollment system, including but not limited to:

- Full name
- Date of birth
- Contact information (email address, phone number, home address)
- Educational background
- Family information
- Uploaded documents (e.g., birth certificate, report cards)
- Profile photo

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

- Processing your enrollment application
- Communicating with you about your application status
- Verifying your identity and eligibility
- Improving and optimizing our enrollment system
- Complying with legal obligations

## 4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Neon Serverless Postgres for database storage and Cloudflare R2 for file storage. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

### Infrastructure and Security Measures

Our application employs robust security measures and utilizes industry-leading infrastructure:

- **Cloudflare Protection**: The app is secured by Cloudflare, which provides advanced DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service) protection, ensuring the availability and stability of our services.
- **Vercel Deployment**: We use Vercel as our deployment platform, benefiting from its global CDN and optimized performance.
- **PostgreSQL Database**: Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database, known for its reliability, feature robustness, and performance.

These measures collectively enhance the security, performance, and reliability of our enrollment system.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

- School administrators and staff involved in the enrollment process
- Third-party service providers who assist us in operating the enrollment system
- Government authorities or law enforcement, when required by law

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Correct any inaccuracies in your personal information
- Request deletion of your personal information (subject to our legal obligations)
- Object to or restrict the processing of your personal information

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
