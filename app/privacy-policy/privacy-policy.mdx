# Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Current Date]

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to the St. Vincent School Foundation, Inc. (SVSFI) Online Enrollment System. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our online enrollment system.

## 2. Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) Compliance

This policy complies with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) of the Philippines. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

## 3. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us when using the enrollment system, including but not limited to:

- Full name
- Date of birth
- Contact information (email address, phone number, home address)
- Educational background
- Family information
- Uploaded documents (e.g., birth certificate, report cards)
- Profile photo

## 4. Consent

By using our enrollment system, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

## 5. Use of Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

- Processing your enrollment application
- Communicating with you about your application status
- Verifying your identity and eligibility
- Improving and optimizing our enrollment system
- Complying with legal obligations

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

## 7. User Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act, you have the right to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Correct any inaccuracies in your personal information
- Object to or restrict the processing of your personal information
- Request deletion of your personal information (subject to our legal obligations)

## 8. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

## 9. GDPR Compliance for International Users

For users from the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). In addition to the rights mentioned above, EEA users have the right to:

- Receive a copy of their personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format (data portability)
- Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

## 10. COPPA Compliance

Our enrollment system is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [contact email].

By using the SVSFI Online Enrollment System, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
